Time for another release!

This release covers a variety of updates. Key among them is the addition of Snapcast support, a feature initially inspired by my interest in Music Assistant. Additionally, the release includes updated components, a new French translation, and several bug fixes.

Existing RoPieee users will automatically receive this update on their devices. The latest image for new users can be downloaded directly from [here](https://github.com/RoPieee/RoPieee/blob/main/docs/releases/2026_03.md).

For the changelog, images, and documentation, visit [RoPieee's GitHub page](https://github.com/RoPieee/RoPieee).

Enjoy!
